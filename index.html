Order Income = (Price − Seller Disc − Shopee Disc) + Shipping by Buyer − Actual Ship + Rebate − Fees − Other  •  Net Profit = Order Income − your costs. Rates are yours to edit. Not affiliated with Shopee.

Settings

Preferences apply instantly to the calculation.

Auto-apply 8% SST (Transaction Fee only)
Applies 8% SST to the Transaction Fee rate only — and only when the rate you type is a clean value divisible by 0.5 (e.g. 3.50% → 3.78%). Commission, Cashback and other rates are used exactly as entered (they already include SST). AMS is never affected. This mirrors Shopee Calculator Lite.
Preferred / Star Seller
Adds the free-shipping handling fee at the lower RM0.18 rate instead of RM0.28, added into Total Other.
Preserve settings on Reset
Keeps category, fee %s and options when you press Reset — only clears the amounts.
